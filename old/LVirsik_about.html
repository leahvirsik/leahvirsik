ARTIST STATEMENT

I use bits and parts of material no longer used for their original purpose. Through an act of unmaking, by my own hand or otherwise, the material I use is no longer a castoff, but speaks of potentiality and a remaking of the self. My works formally connect through repetition and the element of the line.

My use of everyday material, such as bedding, clothing and magazines, references domesticity and relationship. Using constraints of space, material or a predetermined rule, each work, built of multiple pieces, comes together to create a unified whole.
